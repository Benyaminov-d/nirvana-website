Sur

 

As a modern AI system synthesises an answer from multiple retrieved documents to better satisfy a query,  Sur synthesises a  search-query-derived composition  from the products surfaced by search, with a view to align more closely with your selected parameters than any single item might. 

What Sur does:

Given your search parameters and the eligible set under the  Nirvana Standard , as ordered by their search relevance, Sur deterministically either selects, where available, a  pre-existing, set  that most closely satisfies the stated search parameters, or assembles a  composition  from multiple eligible items using a  uniform, rules-based recipe .

The objective of Sur is to improve the   relevance of   results to your   search query .

Sur does  not  alter item eligibility and it does  not  advise on any person’s financial circumstances or holdings.  Sur is not a suitability or appropriateness assessment.  There is  no individualisation : given the same parameters and the same snapshots, any two Members receive the same composition. Outputs are  time-stamped  and  commercially neutral . 

What Sur is not:

The Sur composition is an  impersonal, reproducible depiction  for illustration and benchmarking. It is  not  investment advice, a personal or impersonal recommendation, portfolio management, or the arrangement or execution of transactions. Sur does  not  invent instruments or fabricate data; it operates solely on items surfaced under the  Nirvana Standard  for your search query.  Sur does not monitor markets for you, rebalance any arrangement, or place/arrange transactions.
