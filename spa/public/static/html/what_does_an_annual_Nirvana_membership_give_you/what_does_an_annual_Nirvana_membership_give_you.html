What does an annual Nirvana membership give you?

Only Nirvana gives explicit “what’s my bottom line” loss-level estimates to the general public for financial products, updated daily.

This page explains, in clear terms, what your annual membership includes, what it does not include, and how Nirvana operates. It reflects the Member EULA and current programme decisions.

What you receive with your annual membership

1) Check the downside on your existing investments
Enter the names or symbols of investments you already hold (or are considering). For each, Nirvana shows:

i) The expected (average) loss in a down year.
ii) The expected loss across the  worst 5% of years  ( ~1 in 20 years)
iii) The expected loss across the  worst 1% of years  ( ~1 in 100 years).

2) Quantified downside for every eligible result
Set your  loss-limit  (your maximum comfort loss level). Nirvana responds with a filtered set of  eligible results  and a  clear loss-level estimate  for each, so you can see the estimated size of potential downside in investments. This lets you quickly focus on candidates whose  estimated downside aligns  with your limit.

2) Compass Score relevance ranking, tracked and refreshed daily
Results are ordered by the  Compass Score , a search-relevance index that updates each day. You can return any time to check whether how well do search results match your historical search parameters – or set a new search requirement.

3) The Nirvana Standard eligibility gate
Your results include only those items that meet our internal criteria - the  Nirvana Standard,  for your selected search parameters. The Standard is a uniform, rules-based gate. It is impersonal and does not consider any person’s financial circumstances.

4) Access to Sur
When you click “Invoke Sur”, Sur produces an illustrative composite from items that have passed the Nirvana Standard and are ordered by search relevance for your query. The objective is  query relevance only . Sur does  not  assess suitability and does  not  consider any person’s financial circumstances or holdings.  Sur is not a suitability or appropriateness assessment.  Given the same search inputs and the same daily data snapshot, any two Members receive the same result. Outputs are  time-stamped, impersonal analytics .  Any proportions shown are relevance indicators for search ranking, not investable weights, and the composite is not intended for implementation or execution.  Sur does  not create, arrange, manage, monitor, rebalance, place or arrange any financial instrument or transaction.

5) Satya conversational search
Use plain language to articulate objectives, specify a loss limit (for example, −20%), and explore the results that pass the Standard and rank most relevant for your query.

6) Transparent explanations
For each result you see why it appeared, its eligibility status, its loss-level estimate, and its current Compass Score.

6) Account tools
Saved searches, versioned data snapshots, and simple account controls for renewal and cancellation.

7) Member support
Access to support for questions about using the Network, your account, and your billing.

What is not included

  • Personalised advice or recommendations.
  • Portfolio management, brokerage, or order execution.
  • Tax, legal, or accounting advice.
  • Third-party app fees and any à-la-carte services such as  Sur  invocations.

How Nirvana works (in brief)

Neutral search
Nirvana is a neutral search  network . It does not sell in-house products, accept commissions, or run adverts. Outputs are impersonal analytics derived from your selected parameters.

Daily data snapshots
Nirvana updates its searchable data once per day. The latest snapshot timestamp is shown on-site. Identical inputs on the same data snapshot produce identical outputs; values may change when snapshots update.

Compass Score display policy
Scores are shown in  50-point increments . This presentation avoids false precision while preserving comparability across results.

Coverage and releases
Available markets and product types are defined by the current release. Coverage expands over time. A current list of supported markets is available on-site.

Billing and membership terms

Annual membership
Membership is billed  annually in advance  and  auto-renews  until you cancel in your account settings before the renewal date. There are  no refunds  except where required by law.

Price presentation
The annual price is shown at checkout. If you have a valid  Trust Code , a  10% discount  is applied automatically at checkout. Applicable taxes may be added by the Merchant of Record.

Trust Code
Trust Codes provide Members with a 10% discount on the annual subscription. Trust Codes do not apply to  Sur  or other à-la-carte charges unless explicitly stated on-site.

Merchant of Record (MoR)
A licensed Merchant of Record processes payments and issues receipts. The MoR may add applicable taxes during checkout and may send advance renewal notices where required by local law.

Using your membership day-to-day

  1. State your search parameter  in plain language and, if you wish, specify a maximum acceptable loss level (for example, −15%).
  2. Review your results , each of which has cleared the Nirvana Standard and is ranked by Compass Score.
  3. Read the loss-level estimate  for each eligible result to understand potential downside.
  4. Return at any time . Scores refresh daily, so you can re-check relevance for your current query.

Why this is different

Only Nirvana provides explicit loss-level estimates for every eligible result, updated daily , and ranks eligible items by a transparent, daily-refreshed relevance index.
