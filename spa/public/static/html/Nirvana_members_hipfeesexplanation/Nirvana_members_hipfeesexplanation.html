Nirvana membership fees

Your annual membership

  • USD 569 per year  (billed annually in advance).
  • Auto-renews  each year until you cancel in your account before the renewal date.
  • No refunds  except where required by law. Applicable taxes may be added at checkout by our Merchant of Record.

What is included in your membership

  • Access to Nirvana’s neutral search across supported markets.
  • Loss-level estimates  for eligible results in plain English.
  • Compass Score  relevance ranking, updated daily.
  • Satya conversational search, saved searches, and Member support.

(Outputs are impersonal analytics; they are not advice or predictions.)

Optional add-ons (not included in the annual fee)

  • Sur Report:   USD 249 per invocation.  A one-time, timestamped report that synthesises your eligible results into an illustrative composition aligned to your selected parameters and loss-limit. Trust Codes do  not  apply to Sur.
  • Third-party apps  in Firefly: priced separately by each provider.

Discounts and partner codes

  • If you use a  Trust Code , it is applied automatically at checkout. Your price at checkout is  USD 569 .
  • When a Trust Code is used, the issuing organisation receives  10% of your subscription payment  from Nirvana. This does  not  change what you pay.

Taxes, currency, and charges

  • Prices are shown in  USD .
  • Taxes  (for example, VAT/GST) may be added by the Merchant of Record in your location before you complete payment.
  • Nirvana does  not  add any non-optional platform fees to your membership price.

What you are not paying for

  • No brokerage, execution, or transaction fees charged by Nirvana.
  • No commissions and no in-house products.
  • No advertising charges.

Important clarity

  • Nirvana is a  neutral search network . Outputs are  impersonal, model-based analytics , not advice, recommendations, portfolio management, or transaction execution.
  • Results and scores are updated with each  daily data snapshot ; values may change when snapshots update. Scores are shown in  50-point increments .

Our pledge

  • We irrevocably pledge  10% of total consolidated revenue  to the to protect forests and animal habitats and to support humanitarian projects.
